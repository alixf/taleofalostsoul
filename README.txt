10 Seconds
==========

Basics
------

- The Hero is a soul
- It changes color with power-ups
- It goes through mase levels
- The level progression is not linear (there may be several exit per level)
- It must finish each level in 10 seconds
- It is controlled with the mouse (or by touch)

Power ups
---------

- Time : Add time to finish the level
- Speed : The moving speed of the soul is increased
- Shield : During a short duration it can't be hit by enemies

Enemies
-------

Every time the soul take damage, it reduces the time provided to finish the level
- Pink Hedgehogs : Follow the soul and do collision damage
- Towers : Launch AOE movement-predictive damage
- Fire : Can't go through without resist
